Qiblah

The app features a built-in compass that accurately indicates the Qiblah, helping you easily determine the direction towards the Kaaba in Mecca, which is north. This ensures you can perform your prayers facing the correct direction, no matter where you are.

Quran

The app provides the full text of the Quran, allowing users to read and reflect on the holy scripture. With a user-friendly interface and clear, easy-to-read font, it’s perfect for daily recitation and study, offering the convenience of having the Quran always available at your fingertips.

Prayer time

The app displays the five daily prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha) based on your selected sect and location. You can rely on precise prayer times that adjust automatically, ensuring you're always aware of the next prayer throughout the day.

Mosque finder

When you enable location services, the app identifies mosques closest to you, helping you easily find a place to pray when you're on the go. Whether traveling or exploring new areas, you’ll always know where to find the nearest mosque for congregational prayers.
